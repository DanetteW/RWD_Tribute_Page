A Tribute To My Brother

Though the love between siblings will never be perfect, I can't imagine living my life without my little brother.

Whether we bicker or have full on arguments, it will never outweigh the love and yearning I have for him. He is my every reason for waking up in the mornings. To somehow find a way of making up all the time we have lost, is my daily goal. Together we shall build a future the same way we are constantly building our present. No one can take that away from us.

If I had to list the reasons why he deserves an Oscar, not for his extraordinary performance in some movie, but rather for being one hell of a brother and for never backing down to what this life throws at him.
